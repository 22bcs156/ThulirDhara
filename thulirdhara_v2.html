Mithran

Pasumai Mithran – Friend of Green Nature

Mithran is the green guardian and farmer’s friend from Thulir Dhara, helping protect soil, water, and crops through sustainable agricultural innovation.

Origin Story

Long ago, the land began to suffer from chemicals, plastic waste, and soil damage. Farmers struggled as the earth lost its strength. From the first green sprout (Thulir) that grew after a rain, a small guardian was born — Mithran.

Mithran travels across farms helping the soil heal and teaching farmers new sustainable ways to grow food. Wherever Mithran appears, soil becomes richer, crops grow healthier, and waste turns into useful materials.

Visual Identity of Mithran

  • Green body → represents nature
  • Farmer hat → represents agriculture
  • Wind turbine symbol → represents sustainability
  • Friendly expression → builds trust with farmers

Our Story

Technology Meets Tradition

Welcome to ThulirDhara — where technology meets tradition. We believe nature is not just a resource, but a living partner. Our mission is to protect soil, conserve water, and empower farmers through sustainable innovation.

Founded with deep respect for Tamil Nadu's rich agricultural heritage, ThulirDhara bridges the gap between modern agri-tech and traditional farming wisdom.
